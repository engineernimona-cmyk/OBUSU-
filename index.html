About OBUSU

The official representative body for all Oda Bultum University students

Our Mission

To represent and support students while promoting leadership, academic success, and campus engagement through inclusive programs and services.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that empowers every student to reach their full potential and contribute positively to society.

Core Values

  • Student-Centered Advocacy
  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Inclusivity & Diversity
  • Academic Excellence
  • Community Engagement
